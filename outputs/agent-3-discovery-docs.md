## TECHNICAL NOTES

### M3U/HLS Format
- **M3U8 format**: All streams provided use HTTP Live Streaming (HLS) standard
- **Compatible players**: VLC, Kodi, Plex, Tivimate, TiviMate, Smart TV apps
- **Playlist import**: Paste M3U URL directly into IPTV player or download and load locally

### Regional Considerations
- India-based streams may have regional blocks
- Asian streams may have geofencing for non-Asian IPs
- UAE/Middle East streams accessible globally
- French streams may have EU geofencing restrictions

### Quality Standards
- **HD**: 720p resolution (1280x720)
- **Full HD**: 1080p resolution (1920x1080)
- Most documentary streams serve HD quality
- Bitrates vary by source; typically 3-8 Mbps for HD streams
