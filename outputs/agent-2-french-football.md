## RELIABILITY & PERFORMANCE NOTES

### Expected Uptime
- **GitHub playlists**: 70-85% (frequently updated)
- **iptv-org**: 75-90% (curated, volunteer-maintained)
- **Individual stream URLs**: 30-50% (expire frequently)
- **Peak times**: Reduced reliability during live matches

### Quality Factors
- **FHD streams**: Fewer available, higher CPU demand
- **HD streams**: Most stable option
- **SD streams**: Most reliable but lower quality
- **Buffering**: Common during prime time (weekends, match day)

### Regional Restrictions
- **France**: Full access to most French channels
- **EU**: Some geo-blocking possible for certain beIN feeds
- **Outside EU**: VPN may be required for optimal access

## LEGAL DISCLAIMERS

### Copyright Status
- **Many free IPTV streams**: Unauthorized/gray-market distribution
- **Copyright owners**: beIN Sports, RMC Sport, France Télévisions, Eurosport own broadcasting rights
- **Legal alternatives**: Official subscriptions (beIN Sports, RMC Sport), Prime Video, Fubo, DAZN
- **Regional legality**: Varies by jurisdiction; VPN use in some countries may violate terms of service

### Risk Assessment
- **Malware risk**: Moderate (use trusted GitHub sources, avoid sketchy ad-heavy sites)
- **IP blocking**: Possible from ISPs; VPN recommended
- **Account suspension**: IPTV services may flag unusual activity

### Recommendation
For legal peace of mind, consider:
- Official subscriptions when possible
- Free legal options (Eurosport free in France, Fubo trial)
- VPN protection if using free IPTV
