## 3. DEMO SCRIPT — What to Show, In What Order

**Before you approach anyone:** DASH must already be open on your phone, logged in, with a live channel playing. Never fumble with loading in front of a customer.

### The Demo Flow (90 seconds)

**Step 1: SPORTS (30 seconds)**
- Open the Sports section
- Show live football channels. If a match is live, show it playing.
- If no live match, show a sports news channel or highlight channel
- Say: "See? This na live right now. Not recording."
- Scroll through to show how many sports channels exist

**Step 2: MOVIES (20 seconds)**
- Switch to the Movies section
- Show Nollywood movies first (most relatable)
- Then Hollywood
- Say: "Thousands of movies. New ones every week."

**Step 3: SPEED (10 seconds)**
- Tap on something, let it load fast
- Say: "See how fast e dey load? No buffering. Smooth."

**Step 4: KIDS (10 seconds, only if they have kids)**
- Show the Kids section
- Say: "Your pikin dem go love this part. Cartoons, Disney, all safe."

**Step 5: THE HANDOFF (20 seconds)**
- Turn YOUR phone to face THEM
- Let them tap around for 20-30 seconds
- Let them FEEL it. People trust what they touch.

**Step 6: THE CLOSE**
- "Na the same thing you go get for your own phone. Want me set you up? Takes 2 minutes."

### Demo Tips
- ALWAYS test your connection before you start showing people. Know your strong signal spots.
- Show football FIRST. Always. Everything else is bonus.
- If connection slow that day, have a screen recording saved on your phone as backup.
- Let them hold the phone. This is the most important step.
- Keep energy casual, not desperate. You're sharing something good, not begging.
