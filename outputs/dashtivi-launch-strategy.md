## MARKET REALITY CHECK

Before any strategy, here is what we are actually dealing with:

**Sierra Leone by the numbers:**
- Population: ~9 million. Freetown: ~1.4 million.
- Median age: 19.9 years. 42% under 25. This is a YOUTH market.
- Smartphone penetration: 63% of mobile owners have smartphones. Growing 11.3% YoY.
- Mobile money: Orange Money + Afrimoney (Africell) dominate. National Payment Switch enables interoperability.
- Average monthly salary (Freetown): ~SLE 30-50 (formal sector varies wildly; informal is much lower)
- A meal at an inexpensive restaurant: SLE 0.70-3.00
- Mobile data 1GB/month (Africell): SLE 20. Weekly 500MB: SLE 10. Daily 260MB: SLE 5.20
- Exchange rate: 1 USD = ~24 SLE (New Leone, post-2022 redenomination)
- WhatsApp open rate: 83% daily. THE communication channel.

**Competition:**
- DStv Premium: ~$52/month (SLE 1,248). Compact: ~$19/month (SLE 456). Requires decoder + dish + installation.
- StarTimes: ~$3-15/month. Requires decoder. 30,000+ dealers across Africa.
- Pirated content: USB sticks with downloaded movies SLE 2-5. Shared IPTV passwords. Free football streams on phones.
- Showmax: ~$4-6/month. Limited sports.

**The opening:** DStv is expensive and requires hardware. StarTimes is cheap but limited and requires hardware. Pirates are unreliable and have no live sports. NOBODY is offering a phone-first, no-hardware, premium live TV + movies experience at an affordable price. That gap is DashTivi+.

## 3. REFERRAL SYSTEM (VIRAL LOOP)

### The Core Mechanic

Every DashTivi+ user gets a unique referral code. Format: `DASH-[username]` (e.g., DASH-AMINATA, DASH-IBRAHIM).

When someone signs up using your code:
- **You get**: 3 free days added to your current plan
- **They get**: Free 24-hour Match Pass (their "first match free")
- **If they buy a Weekly or Monthly within 7 days**: You get an additional 3 free days (6 total)

### Why This Works

**Tested pattern from Wave, Chipper Cash, TapTap Send in West Africa:**
- The reward must be IMMEDIATE and TANGIBLE. "3 free days" is instant -- you see your expiry date extend the moment your friend signs up.
- The reward must benefit BOTH parties. One-sided rewards feel exploitative. Both-sided rewards feel like sharing.
- The trigger event must require REAL engagement. Just signing up is not enough -- the friend must actually USE the service (watch something) for the bonus to count. This prevents fake account abuse.

### WhatsApp-Native Referral Flow

This is the primary referral channel. Here is the exact flow:

1. User taps "Share" in the DashTivi+ app
2. It generates a WhatsApp message:

```
Eh bo, you for try DashTivi+! I dey watch Premier League, Champions League, movies -- everything on my phone. No decoder, no dish.

Your first match is FREE. Use my code: DASH-AMINATA

Download here: tivi.dasuperhub.com

If you sign up, both of us get 3 extra days free!
```

3. The message is pre-written in Krio (not English -- this is critical for SL market)
4. User taps send, picks WhatsApp contacts or groups
5. Friend clicks link, enters code, gets free 24 hours
6. Both get rewarded

### Referral Leaderboard: "DASH Ambassador" Programme

**Bronze Ambassador**: 5 referrals -- Free Monthly subscription
**Silver Ambassador**: 15 referrals -- Free Quarterly subscription + "Silver" badge visible on profile
**Gold Ambassador**: 50 referrals -- Free annual subscription + exclusive "Gold" profile frame + early access to new features
**Diamond Ambassador**: 100 referrals -- Free lifetime subscription + SLE 50 cash monthly + invitation to DASH events + name on "Founders Wall" in the app

The leaderboard is PUBLIC. Top referrers are shown in the app. Monthly leaderboard reset with a "Referrer of the Month" spotlight that gets shared on DashTivi+ social media.

**Why status works in SL**: Social status is currency. Being seen as someone who "knows things first" and can get people access is deeply valued. The Ambassador programme turns your best customers into micro-celebrities within the app.

### Anti-Abuse Measures

**Problem**: People will create fake accounts to refer themselves.

**Solutions:**
1. **Phone number verification**: One account per phone number. No duplicate numbers.
2. **Device fingerprinting**: Track device ID. One referral reward per unique device.
3. **Activity requirement**: The referred user must watch at least 30 minutes of content within 48 hours for the referral to count. This eliminates drive-by fake signups.
4. **Velocity limits**: Maximum 5 referral rewards per day per user. If someone suddenly "refers" 20 people in an hour, flag and review.
5. **Same-network detection**: If referrer and referee are on the same WiFi network or same IP, flag for review (likely same person with two phones).
6. **Grace period**: Referral bonuses are "pending" for 24 hours before being applied. If the referred account is flagged, the bonus is cancelled.

**The honest truth**: Some abuse will happen. Budget for 10-15% leakage. The viral growth from legitimate referrals will far outweigh the cost of some free days given to gaming users. Do not over-engineer anti-abuse to the point where legitimate referrals become frustrating.

## 5. TRUST BUILDING

### 5.1 The Trust Problem

Sierra Leone has been burned by digital scams. Lassa apps, fake lottery schemes, Ponzi-structured "investment" apps. When someone sees a new digital product, their default reaction is suspicion. You must overcome this BEFORE you can sell.

### 5.2 Trust Signals (in order of impact)

**1. Local phone number for support**

Not email. Not a chatbot. A real Sierra Leonean phone number that people can call or WhatsApp.
- Get an Africell number: +232 XX XXXXXX
- Staff it with one person (part-time, SLE 100-150/month) who speaks Krio and English
- WhatsApp Business profile with logo, hours, and auto-replies
- Response time target: under 30 minutes during operating hours (8AM-10PM)
- This alone separates DashTivi+ from every other digital product

**2. Physical presence**

Even a small kiosk matters enormously. It says: "We are real. We are HERE."
- Phase 1 (Month 1-3): No physical office needed. Agents ARE the physical presence.
- Phase 2 (Month 4-6): One branded kiosk at a major junction (PZ area or Lumley). Cost: SLE 200-400/month rent + branding.
- Phase 3 (Month 7+): Small office/shop in central Freetown. Meeting point for agents, customer walk-ins, and brand presence.

**3. Testimonial videos**

Real users, filmed on phone, in Krio. 30-60 seconds.
- "My name is Ibrahim, I live in East End. I used to pay SLE 500 for DStv. Now I watch everything on my phone for SLE 25. DashTivi+ is real."
- Collect 5-10 of these in the first month from early adopters
- Share on WhatsApp Status, Facebook, and TikTok
- These are more powerful than any professional ad

**4. Orange Money / Afrimoney payment**

The mere fact that you accept payment through established mobile money platforms is a trust signal. People trust Orange Money. They trust Afrimoney. If THOSE platforms accept DashTivi+, then DashTivi+ must be legitimate.

Display "Pay with Orange Money" and "Pay with Afrimoney" logos PROMINENTLY on all marketing materials.

**5. Known figure endorsement**

You do not need a celebrity endorsement deal (expensive, often fake). You need:
- 2-3 local micro-influencers with 5K-20K followers on Instagram/TikTok
- Cost: Free DashTivi+ subscription + SLE 20-50 per post
- Target: Young Sierra Leoneans known for entertainment/lifestyle content
- 1 local football personality or sports commentator
- 1 popular barber or salon owner who is already on social media

**6. "Money back guarantee"**

Does this concept work in Sierra Leone? Partially. Here is the adapted version:

"If DashTivi+ does not work on your phone, your agent will refund you on the spot."

This is not a blanket satisfaction guarantee (abuse would be rampant). This is a TECHNICAL guarantee: if the app genuinely does not work on your device, you get your money back. This removes the #1 purchase barrier: "What if it does not work on my phone?"

Agents carry a small float for refunds. In practice, the PWA works on any smartphone with a browser, so refund rate should be under 2%.

**7. Consistency over time**

The biggest trust builder is simply BEING THERE. In SL, many digital products appear and vanish. Every month that DashTivi+ is still working, still streaming, still responding to support messages, trust compounds. Month 1 is skepticism. Month 3 is curiosity. Month 6 is trust. Month 12 is loyalty. Plan for the long game.

## 6. PSYCHOLOGICAL POSITIONING

### 6.1 Scarcity (Use Sparingly)

**"Limited codes in your area"**

Do NOT use blanket artificial scarcity -- Sierra Leoneans will see through it. Instead, use real scarcity:
- "The first 100 signups in East End get their first Weekly free" -- real number, real deadline
- "Match Pass codes are limited during Champions League final" -- plausible (server capacity), creates urgency
- Agent-level scarcity: "I only have 5 free passes left this week" -- personal, credible

### 6.2 Social Proof (Use Heavily)

This is the primary psychological lever. In a collectivist culture, people do what other people do.

- **Counter in the app**: "2,347 people watching right now in Freetown" (real-time viewer count)
- **Neighborhood proof**: "147 people in East End are watching this match" (location-based)
- **Agent social proof**: "Join 50 other customers who signed up with Agent Fatmata this month"
- **WhatsApp proof**: Screenshots of happy customers posted to status. "My customer from Kissy dey enjoy Champions League right now"

### 6.3 Status (The Hidden Lever)

Status matters deeply in Sierra Leone's social fabric. DashTivi+ is not just entertainment -- it is a marker of being modern, connected, and ahead.

- **"DASH Member" badge**: Visible on profile, shareable as a WhatsApp sticker
- **Share what you're watching**: "Ibrahim is watching Manchester United vs Chelsea on DashTivi+" -- shareable card
- **Quarterly/Annual subscribers get "Premium Member" frame** for their profile picture
- **Ambassadors get public recognition**: Name in the app, social media shoutout

Positioning: DashTivi+ users are not "subscribers." They are "members." Membership implies belonging. Subscription implies obligation.

### 6.4 FOMO (Football-Specific)

Live football is the ONLY content that cannot be time-shifted. A movie can wait. A series can wait. A Premier League match is happening RIGHT NOW, and if you do not have DashTivi+, you are the only one at the junction not watching.

Exploit this:
- **Push notifications 30 minutes before major matches**: "Arsenal vs Chelsea kicks off in 30 minutes. Do you have your Match Pass?"
- **WhatsApp status from agents during matches**: Video of live stream + "If you no get am, you dey miss" in Krio
- **Post-match content for non-subscribers**: "Here is what happened in the match you missed. Do not miss the next one."

### 6.5 Anchoring (DStv Comparison)

Every piece of marketing should include the DStv comparison:

"DStv charges SLE 456/month and you need to buy a decoder for SLE 1,500. DashTivi+ gives you MORE channels, MORE movies, and live football for SLE 25/month. No decoder. No dish. Just your phone."

The anchor is not "DashTivi+ costs SLE 25." The anchor is "DStv costs SLE 456 and DashTivi+ costs SLE 25." The brain processes it as "I am saving SLE 431."

### 6.6 Reciprocity (The Gift Strategy)

When someone gives you something, you feel obligated to reciprocate.

- The "first match free" is a gift. Not a trial -- a GIFT from the person who referred you.
- Agents giving demos are giving their time. Customers feel compelled to "give back" by signing up.
- Community viewing events are free. Attendees feel they owe loyalty.

### 6.7 Community (The Belonging Play)

"Join the DASH family" -- not "subscribe to DashTivi+."

- Create a DashTivi+ WhatsApp Community (the Meta feature that allows broadcast to multiple groups)
- Members get: match day alerts, exclusive content previews, behind-the-scenes, polls ("Who will win tonight?")
- This community becomes self-reinforcing. Members recruit other members because they want their friends in the group.

## 8. ETHICAL PERSUASION VS MANIPULATION

### What Is Acceptable in Sierra Leone Market Norms

Sierra Leoneans are pragmatic consumers. They expect:
- Direct selling. An agent walking up and pitching is normal and welcomed.
- Price negotiation. They may ask for a discount. "SLE 8 is my best price" is a respected answer.
- Social selling. Recommendations from friends and community leaders carry weight.
- Demonstration before purchase. "Show me first" is standard.

### Dark Patterns to AVOID

1. **Auto-renewal without clear notice**: NEVER auto-charge someone's mobile money without a clear warning. In Sierra Leone, unauthorized charges destroy trust permanently and spread through WhatsApp groups within hours. Always send a reminder 24 hours before expiry with a clear opt-in: "Your plan expires tomorrow. Reply YES to renew."

2. **Hidden charges**: The price shown is the price paid. No "service fees," no "processing charges," no hidden data surcharges. People talk. One person getting charged SLE 30 when the price says SLE 25 will poison your brand in that entire neighborhood.

3. **Forced subscriptions**: Never require a monthly commitment to access daily passes. The whole pricing architecture is designed for flexibility. Respect that.

4. **Guilt-tripping on cancellation**: "Are you SURE you want to leave?" -- NO. If someone wants to cancel, make it one tap. The exit experience is as important as the entry experience, because a graceful exit leads to a return. A manipulative exit leads to a public complaint.

5. **Fake scarcity**: "Only 3 spots left in your area!" when there is no limit. Sierra Leoneans have finely tuned BS detectors from years of political promises and scam attempts. Fake scarcity damages credibility.

6. **Data harvesting**: Do not require unnecessary personal information to sign up. Phone number + name is enough. Do not sell user data. Do not spam contacts from someone's phone book.

### Persuasion Techniques That Build Long-Term Trust

1. **Transparent pricing**: Show ALL plans. Show the math. "SLE 25/month = SLE 0.83/day = less than a sachet of water per day." When people see you have nothing to hide, they trust you.

2. **Genuine scarcity**: Limited-time promotions tied to real events. "Champions League Final Special: SLE 5 Match Pass instead of SLE 2... wait, that is more expensive. Let me fix: SLE 1 Match Pass for the final." Real value, real deadline.

3. **Social proof from REAL people**: Not stock photos. Not fake testimonials. Real Sierra Leoneans filmed on phone in Krio. The more raw and authentic, the more believable.

4. **The "watch for free" demo**: Agents showing live content on their phones is the most ethical and effective persuasion technique. You are not promising anything -- you are literally showing the product working in real-time.

5. **Easy downgrade**: If someone cannot afford Monthly, do not push. Say: "The Weekly is SLE 8, and the Match Pass is SLE 2. Start with what works for you." The customer who starts at SLE 2 and upgrades to SLE 25 over three months is worth more than the customer you pressured into SLE 25 who churns after one month.

### Handling Churn Without Being Aggressive

When a user's plan expires:
- **Day 0**: "Your DashTivi+ plan has expired. Renew anytime at tivi.dasuperhub.com"
- **Day 3**: "There is a match this weekend. Grab a Match Pass for SLE 2 so you do not miss it." (Only send if there IS a relevant match)
- **Day 7**: Nothing. Silence. Give them space.
- **Day 14**: "We miss you. Here is a free 24-hour pass. Come back anytime." (One-time re-engagement)
- **After Day 14**: No more messages. They know DashTivi+ exists. They will come back when they are ready.

NEVER send more than 3 messages after expiry. NEVER make someone feel bad for leaving. The goal is to be the product they WANT to come back to, not the product that harasses them.

## 10. THE UNFAIR ADVANTAGE

DashTivi+ has advantages that DStv and StarTimes cannot replicate:

1. **No hardware**: The biggest barrier to TV in Sierra Leone is the decoder/dish cost. DashTivi+ needs zero hardware. The smartphone they already own IS the device.

2. **Micro-pricing**: DStv cannot offer a $0.08 daily pass. Their business model requires expensive satellite infrastructure and minimum monthly commitments. DashTivi+ can price at ANY level.

3. **PWA distribution**: No app store approval needed. No download size barrier. Users go to a URL and tap "Add to Home Screen." An agent can set this up in 30 seconds.

4. **Mobile money native**: DStv still relies on bank payments and dealer networks for subscription renewal. DashTivi+ payment is a 10-second mobile money transaction.

5. **WhatsApp-native marketing**: DashTivi+ marketing is a message from a friend. DStv marketing is a billboard. In Sierra Leone, the friend wins every time.

6. **Content advantage**: 4,400 live channels vs DStv Compact's 140. 60,000 movies vs StarTimes' limited library. The content volume is not comparable.

7. **Speed to adapt**: DStv is a corporation with quarterly strategy reviews. DashTivi+ can change pricing, add features, and respond to feedback in 24 hours.
